About Sport Connect

We are the platform that connect different sportsmen together at a particular place to play their favorite sports. We link them with partners that play your favorite sports. And also render various sport advertisements.

Sportsconnect is a platform that connect sportsmen of like mind together to play their favorite sports. We link them together through our online platform thereby matching them to those who need another partners to play with, it is our responsibility to get a particular place for them so that they can achieve their heart desire. Our company is small start-up company with about 4 people. The idea is to satisfy our customers’ needs through our platforms by providing them where to play their desired sports and who to play with. As we all know some sports cannot be played alone it requires you to have a partner to play with which is our main function. We also specialize in advertisement of various sports products, sport medias.
